Super soft Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tbs coconut flour
  • 4 tbs ground almonds
  • 3 tbs rice flour
  • 3 tbs sugar
  • 100 ml coconut-rice-drink
  • (optional) vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together
  2. WHisk eggs and milk into the mixture
  3. Pour some of the batter in a hot pan with molten coconut oil
  4. Bake till first side is brown, then flip the pancake
