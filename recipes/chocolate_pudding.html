Chocolate Pudding

Ingredients

  • 300 ml coconut milk
  • 300 ml coconut-rice-drink
  • 50 g potato starch
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 50 g sugar
  • 2 Ttbs cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Mix 3 tbs of coconut-rice-drink with starch, egg yolks and cocoa powder
  2. Bring the rest of the liquid ingredients with sugar to a boil
  3. Take pot away from heat and whisk in the mixture of egg and starch into the hot liquid
  4. Bring pot back to heat and let the mixture boil for one minute, whilst constant whisking
